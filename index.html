Lakiitu.

Lakiitu is a unified gaming frontend designed to bring emulation, cloud gaming, media, and community into a single, consistent experience. Instead of switching between multiple launchers, platforms, and interfaces, Lakiitu centralizes everything into one smooth, console-like environment.

All-in-One Platform

Lakiitu acts as a central hub for your gaming life. It is designed to work across devices and platforms while remaining fast, clean, and controller-friendly. The goal is to remove friction and let you focus on playing, discovering, and sharing games instead of managing tools.

RetroAchievements

Earn achievements in classic games just like modern titles. RetroAchievements integration allows you to track progress, compare scores, and challenge yourself across supported retro systems while keeping everything synchronized in one place.

Musiic

Musiic is Lakiitu’s dedicated music experience. Stream video game soundtracks and curated game music libraries while browsing, customizing your profile, or chatting with the community — without interrupting your flow.

Cloud Gaming

Play instantly without downloads using cloud gaming services such as Xbox Cloud Gaming and NVIDIA GeForce NOW. Lakiitu integrates these platforms directly into the interface, making high-end gaming accessible from almost any device.

Community

Create a profile, customize themes, use stickers, and connect with other players. Communities are built directly into Lakiitu to encourage sharing, discussion, and discovery around the games you love.

Lensmen AI Helper

Need a guide? Lensmen AI is your contextual companion built into the quick menu. Inspired by the watchful eye of Lakiitu, it automatically detects exactly what game you are playing to provide instant tips, walkthroughs, or assistance when you are stuck.
